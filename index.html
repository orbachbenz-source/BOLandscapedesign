About

Design with
intention

I'm Ben Orbach, an independent landscape designer and Bachelor of Landscape Architecture candidate at the University of Guelph. Based out of Toronto, I'm building BOLD, focused on designing with a unique and fresh perspective.

I believe each landscape is part of something bigger, connecting and interacting with your home, neighborhood, or city, and always people. User-centric and site-cohesive design are at the forefront of my work and I look forward to helping you bring your BOLD vision to life.
