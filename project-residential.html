Overview

A garden built for living

Project TypeResidential Garden
ProgrammePrivate Backyard
ApproachPlanting Design · Spatial Planning

This residential backyard project centres on transforming an underutilised outdoor space into a layered, functional garden that feels like a natural extension of the home. The design establishes a clear hierarchy of spaces — from a paved entertaining terrace to a softly planted transition zone, leading to a wilder, more naturalistic rear garden.

The planting plan is structured around seasonal interest and textural variety, using a mix of perennials, ornamental grasses, and woody shrubs to create year-round character. Attention was paid to privacy screening, shade provision, and low-maintenance management.
