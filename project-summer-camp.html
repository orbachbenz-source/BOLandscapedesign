Overview

Where nature meets programme

Project TypeRecreational Landscape
ProgrammeSummer Camp Grounds
ApproachSite Planning · Ecology

This project involved a comprehensive landscape design for a summer camp facility, balancing the demands of a high-activity recreational programme with a sensitive ecological setting. The design responds to the site's natural topography and existing vegetation, weaving pathways, gathering nodes, and activity zones into the landscape rather than imposing upon it.

Key considerations included stormwater management, accessible circulation, and the creation of distinct spatial experiences — from open meadow gatherings to intimate woodland clearings. The planting palette draws entirely from native and naturalized species, reinforcing a sense of place and supporting local biodiversity.
